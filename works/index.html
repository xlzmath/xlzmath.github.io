Research

I work on the linearly edge reinforced random walk, on the lattice. It is closely related to the Vertex reinforced jump process and a super-symmetry sigma model (the $H^{2|2}$-model). I am also interested in statistical mechanics, such as percolation (finite size criteria, noise sensitivity), polymer or sand pile model (limit shape), random band matrices (Anderson localization).
